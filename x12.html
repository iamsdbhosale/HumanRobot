नववर्षं नूतना आशा, हर्षं मंगलमङ्गलम्।
भिन्नाः पन्थानः सन्त्येव, एकं दीपः प्रकाशते॥
गुढी तिष्ठति सुखाय, ईद् हास्यं वितन्वते।
भेदं विना हि सन्तोषः, सर्वत्रैव प्रकाशते॥
Transliteration:
Navavarṣaṁ nūtanā āśā, harṣaṁ maṅgalamaṅgalam।
Bhinnāḥ panthānaḥ santyeva, ekaṁ dīpaḥ prakāśate॥
Guḍhī tiṣṭhati sukhāya, īd hāsyaṁ vitanvate।
Bhedaṁ vinā hi santoṣaḥ, sarvatraiva prakāśate॥
Meaning in English:
🌸 The New Year brings new hopes, joy, and prosperity.
✨ Though paths may be different, the light of happiness is one.
🏵️ Gudhi stands tall for prosperity,
🌙 Eid spreads smiles and togetherness.
💖 True happiness shines everywhere, beyond all differences!
The New Year brings fresh hope, joy, and auspiciousness.
Though paths may be different, the same light shines for all.
Gudhi (flag) stands for prosperity, Eid spreads laughter and happiness.
Without divisions, true joy radiates everywhere.
💡 Moral:
"Festivals may be different, but joy, unity, and celebration bring us all together!" 🙂🎊
